BENEFITS OF EXERCISING

Exercising has so many benefits that can truly improve your overall well-being. For starters, it’s great for your heart and circulation, helping to lower blood pressure and reduce the risk of cardiovascular problems. Plus, physical activity releases endorphins, which can really boost your mood, reducing stress, anxiety, and even symptoms of depression. You also get the bonus of better sleep, since regular movement helps you fall asleep faster and enjoy more restful nights.
